Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to VortexHelp ("we", "our", "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data you share with us while using our website and online tools.

2. Information We Collect

VortexHelp's tools run directly in your browser. Most of our tools (such as Image Compressor, Word Counter, JSON Formatter, Unit Converter, and Age Calculator) process your data locally on your device and we do not upload, store, or transmit your files or text to any server.

We may automatically collect limited technical data such as your browser type, device type, and general location (country/city level) for analytics purposes.

3. Cookies

We use cookies to improve user experience and may use third-party cookies for advertising purposes (such as Google AdSense). These cookies help display relevant ads and measure ad performance. You can disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

4. Third-Party Advertising (Google AdSense)

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on VortexHelp. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

5. Data Security

Since our tools process data locally in your browser, your files and text never leave your device unless you choose to download or share the output yourself.

6. Children's Privacy

VortexHelp does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can remove it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please visit our Contact Page.
